The Look and Feel of Intercode
==============================

The overall look and feel should be modern and clean. We will avoid gradients and any 3d.
The website should be friendly and approachable for the user. 

Colours
========
For Codevert, our colours represent the simplicity and plesantry of the product.
These are our color swatches:

- #5680E9
- #84CEEB
- #5AB9EA
- #F4F5FF
- #8860D0

Iconography
===========
We will focus on using icons that have a 'solid' design, which adheres to the overall look and feel.

Typography
==========
We will be using Montserrat from Google Fonts to compliment the look and feel.

Links
=====
Links will be bolded and not be underlined until hovered over.

Buttons
=======
Buttons will be a flat design with the chosen colours above.
